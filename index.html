Welcome to Tenki no Haan

Welcome to Tenki no Haan, your go-to weather app inspired by the enchanting world of anime.
The name "Tenki no Haan" combines the Japanese word "tenki" or 天気 (meaning weather) with the name
of the app's creator, Raihaan. This unique name reflects the harmonious blend of cultural
elements and personal passion that make this app special.

Anime-Inspired Design

Our website draws inspiration from the popular anime "Tenki no Ko" (Weathering with You),
capturing its beautiful and whimsical aesthetic. We aim to provide not just accurate weather
forecasts but also an engaging and visually appealing experience.

Accurate and Reliable Weather Information

Stay informed with up-to-date weather data tailored to your location. Tenki no Haan offers
detailed 24-hour forecasts in 2-hour intervals. Whether you're planning your day, week, or a
special event, our app ensures you have the most accurate weather information to stay prepared.

User-Friendly Interface

Navigating through Tenki no Haan is a breeze. Our intuitive design ensures that you can
quickly access the information you need without any hassle. The anime-themed visuals add a touch
of fun and creativity to your daily weather checks.
